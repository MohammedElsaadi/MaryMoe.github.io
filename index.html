About MaryMoe

A story of a man and his Queen

Separated by ten seas but always together in heart.

Maryam is a cat princess with unparalleled beauty and a mind so smart.

She has gorgeous eyes that sparkle and shine.

She has a perfect smile with all her teeth aligned.

She has many talents and never fails.

She has luscious hair and pretty nails.

Her man adores her and has much to learn.

The flame in his heart, for her will always burn.

He loves her so much and seeks to show his appreciation.

But expressing his feelings requires difficult translation.

So he turned to his skills and built her this monument of his devotion.

As they await the day, where no longer will they be separated by an ocean.
