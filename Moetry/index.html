Greatest Reward

When you smile my world stops

When you smile my heart drops

I am stupified by your lips

my brain goes to mush

and just like an eclipse

it really makes me blush

when it's there by my action

I feel such satisfaction

Your Smile

In my heart there are lost ships

but when you laugh, smile, or talk

a lighthouse glows behind your lips

for the ships to find the dock

Your Eyes

Your eyes are so round

so wonderfully dark and brown

that they catch light are sparkle and shimmer

I find myself lost in their glimmer

Your Lessons

Every day of my life feels enriched

as I learn from you and grow

and together these lessons are stiched

into a quilt of all the things I now know

you teach me how to cook

you teach me lessons in love

if each lesson was a chapter of a book

then it would grow and tower above

Shared Memories

There are so many places

that we wish to go and see

there are so many faces

that will see you with me

there is a whole world out there

and it all seems so far away

but think of all the memories we will share

as we discover the world together one day
